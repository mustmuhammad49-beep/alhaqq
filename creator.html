How It Works

From Research to Published — Automated

The Al-Haqq Creator Studio pipeline handles every step between your live session and a polished, captioned, published short.

🎙️
STEP 01

Record Your Live

Go live debunking myths, responding to challenges, or breaking down Quranic arguments. The studio works from any recorded session.

✂️
STEP 02

Auto-Extract Best Moments

AI transcribes your session, identifies the strongest 45–90 second clips, and cuts them with silence removal — no manual editing.

🎬
STEP 03

Polish & Caption

Word-level captions with gold-highlighted key words are burned in automatically. Title banner, background music, and 9:16 formatting applied.

🚀
STEP 04

Publish to TikTok

Polished clips are published directly to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API — no manual uploading required.

Whisper transcription + Claude analysis identify your strongest moments automatically from any length recording.
